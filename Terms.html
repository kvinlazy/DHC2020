General Terms and Conditions for the DHC2020 registration

DHC2020 Conference registration will be opened between 13.04.2020 and 07.09.2020

Instructions for registrants

  1. Paper presenters must register within two weeks after the acceptance of the paper to receive the discounted fee.
  2. Accompanied persons may register through the registration category (General, Presenter, Student) of the principal applicant. They will have access to all the facilities available to the principal applicant

Acknowledgement

  1. Registration and Payment will be confirmed by email.

Cancellation

  1. For cancelation received before 30th of June 2020, registration fee will be refunded with £ 60 cancelation fee deducted.
  2. Cancelation received between 1st of July and 31st of August 2020 will be eligible for refund of 50% of the paid registration fee.
  3. For cancelation after 1st of September 2020 no refunds can be made
  4. Bank administration fee and credit card fee will not be refunded
