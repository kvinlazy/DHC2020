General Terms and Conditions for the DHC2018

Registration fee

£ 630.00 General Participants

£ 450.00 Presenters (Including Poster Exhibitors)

£ 380.00 Students

Registrants of all type completed registration by May 31, 2020, receive £100.00 deduction from the registration fee.

Instructions for registrants

  • Presenters must register after the acceptance of the paper in order to receive the discounted registration fee.
  • Currently registered students from the Academia can also be registered.
  • Accompanying Persons may register through the registration category (General, Presenters or Students) of the principal applicant. They will have access to all facilities availed by the principal applicant as well as special tours if available.

Acknowledgement

Registration and payment will be confirmed by E-Mail.

Cancellation policy

  • For cancellations received before June 30, 2020 registration fee will be refunded with a 100 GBP cancellation fee deducted.
  • Cancellations received from July 1 through August 31, 2020 will be eligible to a refund of 50% of the paid registration fee.
  • For cancellations occurring after September 1, 2020 no refunds can be made.
  • Bank administration fee and credit card fee will not be refunded.
